A project created by Avishek Laudary

This project empowers businesses in Nepal to provide temporary Wi-Fi access, specifically designed to facilitate online payments. As online payment methods are rapidly gaining popularity in the country, this initiative aims to alleviate the hassles associated with Wi-Fi and data connectivity. By offering short-term Wi-Fi access, businesses can enhance customer convenience, allowing seamless transactions without the need for customers to rely on their mobile data. This innovative solution not only promotes cashless payments but also helps businesses attract more customers by creating a smoother, hassle-free shopping experience.
